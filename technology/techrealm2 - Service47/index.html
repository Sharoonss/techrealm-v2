Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical turnaround time for graphic design projects?

The turnaround time for graphic design projects can vary depending on the complexity and scope of the project. Simple designs or minor revisions may be completed within a few days, while larger projects may require more time. During the initial consultation, we will discuss your project timeline and provide you with an estimated turnaround time based on your specific requirements.

Do you provide revisions for the designs?

Yes, we offer revisions for our designs to ensure that they meet your expectations. After the initial design concept is presented, you have the opportunity to provide feedback and request revisions. We value your input and strive to create designs that align with your vision. The number of revisions included may vary depending on the package or project, and any additional revisions beyond the included limit may be subject to an additional fee.

What file formats will I receive for the final designs?

We provide the final designs in various file formats, depending on your needs. Common file formats include JPEG, PNG, and PDF. If you require specific file formats or variations, such as vector files (AI, EPS, or SVG), we can accommodate those requests as well. We ensure that you receive the appropriate file formats that are compatible with both print and digital platforms, allowing you to use your designs seamlessly across different mediums.

How do you ensure the confidentiality of my project and sensitive information?

We take the confidentiality of your project and sensitive information seriously. We have strict internal policies and procedures in place to safeguard your data. Our team members sign non-disclosure agreements (NDAs) to ensure the confidentiality of your project.

What are your pricing and payment terms?

Our pricing is tailored to the specific services and scope of your project. We offer competitive rates based on industry standards and the level of expertise required for your project.
